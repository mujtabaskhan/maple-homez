About Us

Maple Homez is a locally owned and operated company specializing in high-quality home renovations and remodeling. We bring expertise, craftsmanship, and affordability to every project, ensuring that homeowners receive top-tier services tailored to their needs. Whether it’s basement finishing, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, home additions, or open-concept designs, we handle every aspect of the renovation process with precision and care.

We proudly serve the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Milton, Pickering, Ajax, and Whitby.

At Maple Homez, we believe that quality housing shouldn’t be a privilege—it should be accessible to all. Our vision is to transform homes and communities by offering premium renovation solutions at affordable costs, making dream homes a reality for everyone.

Contact us today to schedule a free estimate at your home or business.

Kitchen Renovations

Kitchen renovations can be a daunting task, but with the help of Maple Homez, you can transform your kitchen into a beautiful and functional space. Our team of skilled professionals will work with you to understand your needs, preferences, and budget to create a design that meets your exact requirements.

Basement Renovations
